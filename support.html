Getting Started

  1. Install the iPhone App: Download and install the companion app on your iPhone
  2. Install the Watch App: The Watch app will automatically install on your paired Apple Watch
  3. Import Music: Use the iPhone app to import audio files from the Files app
  4. Transfer to Watch: Select tracks and transfer them to your Apple Watch
  5. Play Offline: Open the Watch app and enjoy your music without your phone!

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I import music to the iPhone app?

Open the app and tap the import button. You can select audio files from your iOS Files app, iCloud Drive, or any other file provider.

How do I transfer music to my Apple Watch?

In the iPhone app, select the tracks you want to transfer and tap "Send to Watch". Make sure your Watch is nearby and connected. The transfer happens wirelessly over Bluetooth.

Can I play music on my Watch without my iPhone?

Yes! Once music is transferred to your Watch, you can play it completely offline without your iPhone nearby. Perfect for workouts and runs.

What audio formats are supported?

The app supports common audio formats including MP3, M4A, AAC, and WAV files.

How much storage does the Watch app use?

Storage usage depends on how many tracks you transfer. The app shows available storage on both iPhone and Watch. A typical 3-4 minute song in MP3 format uses about 3-5 MB.

Music not playing on my Watch?

Try these steps:

  • Ensure tracks have been fully transferred to the Watch
  • Check that your Watch is not in Theater Mode or Do Not Disturb
  • Make sure Bluetooth audio is connected (AirPods or other Bluetooth headphones)
  • Restart the Watch app
  • If issues persist, try re-transferring the tracks

Transfer is slow or failing?

Wireless transfers can take time depending on file size. To improve transfer speed:

  • Keep your iPhone and Watch close together
  • Ensure both devices are charged (transfers pause on low battery)
  • Keep both apps in the foreground during transfer
  • Transfer smaller batches of songs at a time

System Requirements

  • iPhone: iOS 14.0 or later
  • Apple Watch: watchOS 7.0 or later
  • Bluetooth: Required for transferring music

Known Issues

  • Large file transfers (>50MB) may take several minutes
  • Background transfers may pause if apps are closed
