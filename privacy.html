TL;DR: Music Player App respects your privacy. We don't collect, store, or transmit any personal data. Your music stays on your devices.

Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how Music Player App ("we", "our", or "the app") handles your information. We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about our practices.

Information We Don't Collect

Music Player App is designed with privacy as a core principle. We do not collect, store, transmit, or share any of the following:

  • Personal information (name, email, phone number, etc.)
  • Location data
  • Usage statistics or analytics
  • Device identifiers
  • Music files or metadata
  • Listening habits or preferences
  • Any other personal or identifiable information

How the App Works

Music Player App operates entirely on your local devices:

  • Local Storage: All music files and app data are stored locally on your iPhone and Apple Watch
  • Device-to-Device Transfer: Music transfers between your iPhone and Watch happen directly over Bluetooth using Apple's WatchConnectivity framework
  • No Cloud Services: We do not use any cloud storage or remote servers
  • No Third-Party Services: We do not integrate with any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services

Permissions We Request

The app requests the following iOS permissions to function properly:

  • File Access: To import audio files from your device's Files app
  • Media Library: Optional - to access your music library if you choose to import from it
  • Background Audio: To continue playing music while the app is in the background

These permissions are used only for their stated purposes and do not involve any data collection or transmission.

Data Storage

All data is stored locally on your devices:

  • iPhone: Music files and app data are stored in the app's sandboxed storage
  • Apple Watch: Transferred music is stored in the Watch app's local storage
  • Security: Data is protected by iOS and watchOS security features
  • Deletion: All data is permanently deleted when you uninstall the app

Communication Between Devices

Music transfer between your iPhone and Apple Watch uses Apple's WatchConnectivity framework:

  • Transfer happens directly between your paired devices
  • Data is encrypted by the operating system
  • No data passes through any external servers
  • Transfer only occurs when you explicitly initiate it

Third-Party Access

We do not share any information with third parties because we don't collect any information to share. The app:

  • Does not contain advertisements
  • Does not use analytics services
  • Does not integrate with social media
  • Does not connect to external servers

Children's Privacy

Music Player App is safe for users of all ages. Since we don't collect any personal information, there are no additional privacy concerns for children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

Since we don't collect any personal data, there is no data to:

  • Request access to
  • Request deletion of
  • Request correction of
  • Port to another service

All your data stays on your devices and is under your complete control.
